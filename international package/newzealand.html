Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive Auckland

Welcome to New Zealand. Arrive at Auckland ‘The City of Sails’ and transfer to hotel. Overnight stay in Auckland.

Day 2: Auckland - Waitomo - Rotorua

Check out from hotel and travel south through the Waikato region to arrive at Waitomo for a guided tour of the glowworm caves. Continue to Rotorua this afternoon via Hobbiton Movie Set for a guided tour of this world class attraction. Overnight stay in Rotorua.

Day 3: Rotorua

Today after breakfast proceed to visit Te Puia Thermal Reserve, the Agrodome for a sheep and farm show and Rainbow Springs to view trout and wildlife. Rest of day is at leisure. Overnight stay in Rotorua.

Day 4: Rotorua - Queenstown

This morning check out from hotel & transfer to airport to board a flight for Queenstown. Arrive & transfer to hotel for check in. Overnight stay in Queenstown.

Day 5: Queenstown - Milford Sound - Queenstown

Travel through Lumsden and Te Anau to the Fiordland National Park. Enter the Eglinton Valley and travel through the man made Homer Tunnel to arrive in Milford Sound. Enjoy a cruise on the fjord before returning to Queenstown. Overnight stay in Queenstown.

Day 6: Queenstown

Full day is free for leisure. Overnight stay in Queenstown. Optional: - One can enjoy bungee jumping, jet boating, white water rafting, gondola rides at an additional cost

Day 7: Queenstown – Glacier Region

Journey past Lakes Wanaka and Hawea before turning into the Haast Past and the World Heritage Westland National Park. Continue north to arrive in Fox and Franz Josef Glaciers. Overnight stay in Glacier Region.

Day 8: Glacier Region - Greymouth - Christchurch

Check out from hotel and travel north to Greymouth and board the world famous Tranz Alpine train. Ascend through lush beech forests past the alpine village of Arthur’s Pass before crossing the spectacular Southern Alps. Travel over the fertile Canterbury Plains to arrive in the ‘garden city’ of Christchurch. Overnight stay in Christchurch.

Day 9: Christchurch - Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight or back home.
