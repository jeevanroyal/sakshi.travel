Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Tokyo

Welcome to Japan. Arrive at Narita airport and transfer to the hotel and check in. Rest of the day is free at leisure. Overnight stay in Tokyo.

Day 2: Tokyo - Full day sightseeing

Today after breakfast proceed for Tokyo sightseeing. Visit Tokyo Tower - Hppo-en (tea ceremony) - Enjoy Japanese Style lunch, Visit Imperial Palace Plaza, Niju-bashi Bridge - Sumida River Cruise - Asakusa Kannon, Sensoji Temple & Shopping at Nakamise, drive through Ginza. Overnight stay in Tokyo.

Day 3: Tokyo - Mt Fuji & Hakone Tour

This morning proceed for full day tour to Mt. Fuji & Hakone. Visit Mt. Fuji 5th Station. Enjoy Japanese style lunch. Later enjoy Lake Ashi Cruise, Mt. Komagatake Ropeway. Overnight stay in Tokyo.

Day 4: Tokyo - Kyoto

Today check out from hotel and procceed to Tokyo Station on your own. Board Shinkansen Nozomi 27 depart for Kyoto. Arrive Kyoto Station & head to hotel on your own. Check in to Hotel. Overnight stay in Kyoto.

Day 5: Kyoto – Kyoto & Nara Tour

Today after breakfast proceed for Kyoto & Nara tour. Visit Nijo Castle, Kinkaku-ji Temple, Kyoto Imperial Palace, Enjoy Japanese style lunch, later proceed to Nara and visit Nara Park & Todai-ji Temple (Great Buddha), Kasuga Taisha Shrine, Nara Nagomikan. Overnight stay in Kyoto.

Day 6: Kyoto - Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight or back home.
