Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Istanbul

Welcome in Turkey. Arrive in Istanbul and transfer to your hotel. Rest of the day at leisure. Overnight in Istanbul.

Day 2: Istanbul - City Tour

Enjoy a Full day tour of Istanbul, the only city that straddles two continents! Your tour includes Hippodrome, the place chariot races and competitive athletic events took place during the Roman period. Blue Mosque; a 17th century Ottoman Mosque famous with its six minarets and magnificent interior with blue tiles. Continue to St Sophia – the great Byzantine Basilica built in the 6th century, which remained the largest cathedral in the world for almost a thousand years, it was later converted to a mosque and now serves as museum. Then your tour will take you to Topkapi Palace, residence of Ottoman Sultans housing the richness of 700 years. Finally visit the Grand Bazaar, world famous shopping arcade with over 4,000 shops under one roof. Overnight in Istanbul. (B, L)

Day 3: Istanbul – Cappadocia

Today morning depart for Cappadocia by taking an early domestic flight. After 1 hr flight, your tour guide will meet you at the airport and directly join to tour to visit Kaymakli Underground city, where early Christian lived in fear and faith. You will see the beautiful Pigeon Valley and rock formations amazes you. Overnight in Cappadocia. (B, L, D)

Day 4: Cappadocia – Full day sightseeing tour

Depart your hotel for a Full Day tour in Cappadocia. Visit Goreme Open Air Museum in Goreme Valley to see the early Christian churches decorated with unique frescos depicting various scenes from the Bible. Visit Pasabaglari (Monk’s Valley) to see the world famous fairy chimneys which have unique shape. Later you will visit Avanos, the very well-known pottery and craft center since Hittite times. Enjoy handmade ceramics, fashion design jewelry. Overnight stay in Cappadocia. (B, L, D)

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight or back home. (B)
