Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Seoul

Welcome to Korea. Arrive at airport and transfer to the hotel and check in. Seoul is the capital of Korea with over 600 years of history. It is the heart of Korea’s culture and education as well as politics and economics. Seoul is a world-class city with numerous amenities and shopping districts. Seoul is heaven for passionate foodies. Whatever you want, at any time of day or night, Seoul can provide. Overnight stay in Seoul.

Day 2: Seoul - Full day Palace and Traditional market tour

After your breakfast proceed for full day - Palace and Traditional market tour. Visit Jogye buddhist temple, Blue house, Guard changing ceremony & Gyeongbok Palace, National folk museum, Amethyst factor insadong antiques street, Ginseng center, Namdaemun traditional market, Changdeok palace. Overnight stay in Seoul.

Day 3: Seoul

Day is free for leisure & shopping. Overnight stay in Seoul.

Day 4: Seoul - Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight or back home.
