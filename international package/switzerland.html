Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Zurich - Interlaken

Welcome to Switzerland. Arrive at airport and transfer to Interlaken for your stay. You have rest of day at leisure. Overnight stay in Interlaken.

Day 2: Interlaken - Excursion to Jungfraujoch

Spend the day of fun and excitement for an excursion to Jungfraujoch - which takes you to a height of 3,454 metres to reach Jungfraujoch! , commonly referred to as the ‘top of Europe’. Your journey along the Lütschein takes you to Lauterbrunnen or Grindelwald. From there you transfer to the Wengernalp Railway. The Wengernalp Railway will take you to Kleine Scheidegg beneath the Jungfraujoch with a view of the Eiger North Face. The final train is the Jungfrau Railway. It starts from Kleine Scheidegg and takes you directly to the Jungfraujoch-Top of Europe. Here Switzerland’s most breathtaking scenery awaits you, which can only be reached through a 7.3 km long cog railway tunnel. Capture some memorable experiences in Ice Palace, the Ice Plateau. Overnight stay in Interlaken.

Day 3: Interlaken - Lucerne

Today after breakfast check out from hotel & transfer to Lucerne. Arrive & check in at hotel. Rest of the day is free for leisure. Overnight stay in Lucerne.

Day 4: Lucerne – Excursion to Mt. Pilatus

After early breakfast proceed to visit Mt. Pilatus. (Round trip: Lucerne-Alpnachstad-Mt.Pilatus-Kriens-Lucerne). Mount Pilatus rises high above the city of Lucerne giving wonderful views of Lake Lucerne and the Swiss Alps. At 6,992 ft (2,132 m) high, it has 2 cable cars, the world's steepest cog-railway, hotels, restaurants and lots of outdoor activities. Return to Lucerne. Overnight stay in Lucerne. Optional: - Replace Mt. Pilatus with Mt. Titlis: Additional EUR 25 per person.

Day 5: Lucerne - Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to the airport for your onward flight or back home.
