Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Cairo

Welcome to Egypt. Arrive at Cairo airport and transfer to hotel for your stay. Overnight stay in Cairo.

Day 2: Cairo - Visit Pyramids

This morning proceed for full day tour to visit the Great Pyramids of Giza, Le Sphinx, Memphis and Saqqara. Transfer back to your hotel. Overnight stay in Cairo.

Day 3: Cairo - Aswan - Onboard Cruise (Every Sunday or Wednesday or Friday)

Today after breakfast check out from hotel & transfer to Cairo Airport for your flight to Aswan. Arrive to Aswan Airport then transfer to your Cruise for three nights sailing from Aswan to Luxor. Embarkation and lunch on board the Cruise. Afternoon tour by felucca around the Elephantine Island, Botanical Garden and Aga Khan. Dinner & Overnight on board the Cruise in Aswan. Note: - Cruise departs on every Sunday, Wednesday or Friday.

Day 4: Onboard Cruise

Buffet breakfast on board the Cruise. Later proceed with the visit of Aswan including the High Dam, The Unfinished Obelisk and the temple of Phile. Lunch on board the Cruise and sail to Kom Ombo. Visit the temple of Kom Ombo dedicated to Gods Sobek and Haroeris. Afternoon sail to Edfu. Dinner and overnight on board the Cruise in Luxor.

Day 5: Onboard Cruise

After breakfast on board the Cruise proceed to visit the temple of Edfu, dedicated to the god Horus. Lunch on board during sailing to Esna. Cross Esna lock and sail to Luxor. Dinner and overnight on board the Cruise in Luxor.

Day 6: Onboard Cruise - Luxor - Cairo

Today after breakfast disembarkation and proceed with the visits of the West Bank of the Nile including Two Colossi of Memnon , The temple of Queen Hatshepsut at EL Dier El Bahari and the Valley of Kings. Transfer to Luxor airport for you flight to Cairo, and upon arrival you’ll be met and transferred to your hotel for check in and overnight in Cairo.

Day 7: Cairo - Departure

After breakfast it’s time to check out. Transfer to the George airport for your onward flight or back home.
