Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi – Cochin

Breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast proceed to the airport and depart to Cochin. Arrive Cochin. On arrival you will met by our representative at the airport and transferred to the hotel. Check in at the Hotel (Check in Mid-day) Afternoon, proceed for the sightseeing tour of Cochin. The tour begins from “The Dutch Palace (closed on Fridays)” also known as Mattancherry Palace, built by Portuguese in 1555, which is famous for its murals depicting scenes from the Ramayana and the Puranic legends. Adjacent to the palace is the Jewish Synagogue (closed on Fridays & Saturdays) built in 1568. Also visit the St. Francis Church (closed on Sundays), established by the Portuguese Franciscan friars in 1503. Vasco Da Gama was buried in the courtyard of this European church built in India. Other attractions include the traditional fishing hamlets and the fisher folk in their colorful costumes. The tour also includes a visit to the seaside where one can still see the, nearly a century old Chinese fishing nets, which, are still in use. This Evening, attend a Kathakali dance performance at a local theatre, performed by various artists. Kathakali is a traditional dance form, depicting Indian epics (Ramayana and Mahabharata). The dancers are dressed in very colorful and elaborate costumes. Their faces are painted to depict various characters of these epics. This dance has a lot of facial expressions and brisk, yet articulate body movements.Return to the Hotel for an overnight stay. (D)

Day 2: Day 2: Cochin – Periyar

Breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast Proceed to Periyar (160 KM / 4 hrs.) Arrive Periyar and Check in at the Hotel.An evening boat cruise is the best mode to watch the wildlife from a close distance at the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. The Periyar Lake is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the area. Elephants are the most common wildlife you can see on the shores of the reservoir. Apart from elephants, the sighting of wild boar and sambar deer is also possible. Return to the Hotel for an overnight stay. (B, D)

Day 3: Periyar

Breakfast at the Hotel.Morning visit Spice plantations which is famous for nature walking. You can also spot many unique birds that visit the plantation for food. You can taste fresh spices from the plant. Some plantations provide a tasting lunch in which the food is cooked with the fresh spice from the plantation. The plantation will be divided into sections and each section will be covered with specific plants like vanilla, ginger, cardamom, clove, nutmeg and others. Return to the Hotel. Afternoon at leisure. Overnight at the Hotel. (B, D)

Day 4: Periyar - Alleppey

Breakfast at the Hotel After Breakfast proceed to Allepphey(130 KM / 4 Hrs). Arrive Alleppey and check in at the House Boat. Enjoy the scenes of the Kerala countryside in a backwater cruise -skim past Chinese fishing nets, bowed down paddy fields, local inhabitants engaged in making coir products, rustic homes and swaying coconut palms. The Boat will be anchored at 5.30 PM. Overnight at the House boat. (B, L, D)

Day 5: Cochin – Delhi

Breakfast at the house boat.After Breakfast check out and proceed to Cochin Airport. Arrive Delhi check in at the Hotel for an overnight stay. (B, D)

Day 6: Depart Delhi

Breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast check out from the Hotel. Your wonderful vacation comes to an end! Proceed to Delhi International Airport to connect to your onward flight, with wonderful memories to be cherished for a lifetime. (B)
