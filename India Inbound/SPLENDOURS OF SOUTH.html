Itinerary

Day 1: Chennai

Arrive Chennai, you will be met and received by our Tour Executive. He will introduce you to the driver who will be with you throughout your trip. You will proceed to the Hotel. Check in at the Hotel. Afternoon visit Kapaleeshwar Temple, an ancient Shiva Temple, constructed in pure Dravidian style. The Sri Parathasarathy Temple(Foreigners are not allowed inside) is dedicated to Lord Krishna, built in 8th century during the reign of the Pallavas. And Vadapalani Murugan Temple is among the most popular temples of the capital city and is located in the region of Vadapalani in Chennai. Built about 125 years ago, this temple is considered as an auspicious place to get married and witnesses approximately 7000 marriages every year.Overnight at the Hotel.

Day 2: Chennai - Kanchipuram – Mahabalipuram - Pondicherry

Early Breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast drive to Pondicherry. Enroute visit Kanchipuram the golden city of thousand Temples and one of the seven sacred cities of India. In Kanchipuram visit Kamakshi Amman Temple and Kailanathanadan Temple. Proceed to Mahabalipuram to visit Arjuna’s Penance, Shore temple, and Five Rathas. After the visit proceed to Pondicherry. Arrive Pondicherry check in and overnight at the Hotel.

Day 3: Pondicherry - Chidampuram – Kumbakonam

Early Breakfast at the Hotel. After Breakfast visit Govt museum and Auroville where one can live in peace. The Spherical marble with a crystal inside it and the Meditation Centre is the main attraction. Later drive to Kumbakonam and enroute visit the Chidambaram Nataraja Temple. It depicts Lord Shiva as the Lord of the dance Bharatanatyam. The Temple timings are 06.00 to 12.00 PM. Arrive Kumbakonam and check in at the Hotel.In the evening visit Adhi Kumbeshwara Temple and Sarangabani Temple in Kumbakonam.Overnight at the Hotel.

Day 4: Kumbakonam - Thanjore - Trichy

Breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast proceed to Trichy and enroute visit Tanjore Brahadeeshwara Temple, Maratha Palace, Saraswathy Mahal Library, Art Gallery and Sangeeta Mahal. After the visit, proceed to Trichy. Arrive Trichy check in at the hotel.Evening, visit Srirangam Temple and Rock for Srirangam is famous for its Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple, a major pilgrimage destination for Hindus (especially Srivaishnavites) and the largest temple complex in India.The Rock Fort is a historic fort and temple complex built on an ancient rock. It is located in the city of Tiruchirapalli, which is in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is constructed on a 273-foot high rock. There are two Hindu temples inside the Rockfort, the Uchchi Pillaiyar Koil and The Siva Temple.Overnight at the Hotel.

Day 5: Trichy- Madurai

Breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast proceed to Madurai.Arrive Madurai check in at the Hotel. Afternoon, proceed for a sightseeing tour of Madurai –The primary interest is the Meenakshi Temple,where Dravidian temple architecture may be considered to be at its best. Its Hall of a thousand Pillars is fabulous and is filled with worshippers at all hours of the day. The tour also includes a visit to the Thirumalai Nayak Palace & Thiruparangundram temple.Late evening participate in an Aarti Ceremony at Meenakshi Temple. Return to the Hotel.Overnight at the Hotel.

Day 6: Madurai - Tirunelveli – Kanyakumari

Early breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast Proceed to Kanyakumari. Enroute visit Tirunelveli an ancient city of about 2000 years old and a town of Hoary tradition. Arrive Kanyakumari. Check in at the Hotel.Late afternoon visitKumari Amman Temple, (a temple dedicated to virgin Goddess Parvathi),Vivekananada Rock Memorial, Gandhi Memorial and Thiruvalluvar Statue.Overnight at the Hotel.

Day 7: Kanyakumari – Kovalam

Breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast proceed to Kovalam. ArriveKovalam. Check in at the Hotel. Afternoon Sightseeing of Trivandrum visiting. Padmanabha Swamy Temple SriPadmanabha Swamy Temple sits in the heart of the city of Trivandrum. The residing deity of this temple is Lord Vishnu. The temple is managed by the former royal family of Travancore. The temple is designed beautifully in the Dravidian style.Napier Museum, Chitira Art Gallery: Both Napier Museum and Sree Chithra Art Gallery are situated in the Museum compound, right in the heart of the city, near the zoological park. Napier Museum built in the 19th century, this Indo-Saracenic structure boasts a natural air-conditioning system and houses a rare collection of archaeological and historic artefacts, bronze idols, ancient ornaments, a temple chariot and ivory carvings. Kovalam Beach:Kovalam is an internationally renowned beach with three adjacent crescent beaches. It has been a favourite haunt of tourists, especially Europeans, since the 1930s. A massive rocky promontory on the beach has created a beautiful bay of calm waters ideal for sea bathing.Return to the Hotel & Overnight stay at the Hotel.

Day 8: Kovalam

Breakfast at the Hotel.Full day at leisure for individual activity.Overnight at the hotel

Day 9: Kovalam - Trivandrum - Departure

Breakfast at the Hotel. Check out and transferred to the Airport and Depart.
