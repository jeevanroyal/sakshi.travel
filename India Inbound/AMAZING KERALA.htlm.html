Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive Cochin

On arrival you will met by our representative at the airport and proceed to Munnar (130 KMS. / 4 Hrs.) Arrive Munnar then Check in at the Hotel Grand Plaza OR Similar .Evening at leisure Overnight at the Hotel

Day 2: Munnar

Breakfast at the Hotel After breakfast proceed for a visit the Tea estate garden & Factory and Tea Museum.Return to the Hotel.Afternoon Sightseeing of Munnar.Visiting Devikulam is a panoramic hill station near Munnar. With its beautiful valleys and high majestic mountains, Devikulam is a must visit.Visiting Rajmalai, the tourism zone of the famous Eravikulam National Park at an altitude of 8,983 feet above sea level. Which is home to the rare species of mountain goat, “The Niligiri Tahr”. The National Park houses a large variety of flora and fauna; moreover, the beauty of the park is unparalleled. Overnight at the Hotel

Day 3: Munnar – Periyar

Breakfast at the Hotel After Breakfast Proceed to Periyar.Arrive Paeriyar & Check in at the Hotel Tree Top OR Similar Afternoon visit Spice plantations which is famous for nature walking. You can also spot many unique birds that visit the plantation for food. You can taste fresh spices from the plant. Some plantations provide a tasting lunch in which the food is cooked with the fresh spice from the plantation. The plantation will be divided into sections and each section will be covered with specific plants like vanilla, ginger, cardamom, clove, nutmeg and others. Evening return to the hotel and overnight.

Day 4: Periyar

Enjoy early morning Tea/ coffee.After an early morning Tea / coffee, proceed to Lakr boat cruise is the best mode in Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary to watch the wildlife from a close distance. The Periyar Lake is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the area. Elephants are the most common wildlife you can see on the shores of the reservoir. Apart from Elephants, the sight of Wild Boar and Sambar Deer, macaques and Langurs etc. also possible, depending on your luck. Return to the Hotel for Breakfast Afternoon at leisure.Overnight at the hotel

Day 5: Periyar – Alleppey

Breakfast at the Hotel.After Breakfast proceed to Alleppey Arrive Alleppey and check in at the Lakes & Lagoons House Boat. Lunch will be provided in Houseboat Enjoy the scenes in Kerala country side in a backwater cruise -skim past Chinese fishing nets, bowed down paddy fields, local inhabitants engaged in making coir products, rustic homes and swaying coconut palms. The Boat will be anchor at 5.30 PM Dinner in Houseboat Overnight at the House boat. (All Meals – Lunch, dinner & Breakfast plus Tea / Coffee snacks in the evening & Morning will be served at the House boat)

Day 6: Alleppey – Cochin

Breakfast on the House boat After breakfast check out from the house boat at Alleppey and proceed to Cochin (65 KMS / 2 Hrs.) Arrive Cochin and check in at the Hotel Sealord. Afternoon, proceed for half day sightseeing tour of Cochin. The tour begins from “The Dutch Palace (closed on Fridays)” also known as Mattancherry Palace, built by Portugues in 1555, which is famous for its murals depicting scenes from the Ramayana and the puranic legends. Adjacent to the palace is the Jewish Synagogue (closed on Fridays & Saturdays) built in 1568. Also visit the St. Francis Church (closed on Sundays), established by the Portuguese Franciscan friars in 1503. Vasco Da Gama was buried in the courtyard of this European church built in India. Other attractions include the traditional fishing hamlets and the fisher folk in their colorful costumes. The tour also includes a visit to the seaside where one can still see the, nearly a century old Chinese fishing nets, which, are still in use. Overnight at the Hotel

Day 7: Depart Cochin

Breakfast at the Hotel After Breakfast Proceed to the airport for onward destination
