Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival Chennai

We meet you on arrival and assist with transfer to hotel in Chennai. In the afternoon, we tour this cultural heartland and gateway to South India. Overnight at hotel. (D)

Day 2: Chennai - Mahabalipuram Day tour

We visit Mahabalipuram a beautiful beach resort town with miles of unspoiled beach. It is the birthplace of the art and architecture of South India. We see Rathas (Chariots), Arjuna’s Penance (a bas relief), Krishna Mandapa, the Shore Temple, Mahishasurmardini Cave, Varaha Mandapa and Mandapams. Overnight at hotel in Chennai. (B, D)

Day 3: Chennai - Mysore by Train (500Km/ 8Hrs)

Experience a journey on India’s flagship the Shatabdi Express train to Mysore. Sitting 770m above sea level, Mysore is an easy going city famous for its silk trade and sandalwood and incense centre. The city is steeped in history. The Mysore Palace boasts a kaleidoscope of stained glass, mirrors, gilt and colours. See beautifully carved wooden doors and mosaic floors, as well as exquisite paintings. Half-way up the Chamundi Hill, see the Nandi Bull, a 4.8m monolith carved out of solid rock. Hordes of pilgrims come here throughout the year. Right on top is the 2000 year old Chamundeswari Temple with its typical festive atmosphere. Visit the colourful and aromatic Devraj Fruit and Vegetable market. Check In at hotel for night. (B, D)

Day 4: Mysore - Bengaluru Airport - Cochin

Our three-hour drive takes us to Bengaluru Airport for a short flight to Cochin. After arrival at Cochin Airport, check in at room and overnight at hotel in Cochin. (B, D)

Day 5: Cochin - Alleppey (60Km/ 1.5Hrs)

Breakfast at the hotel.After breakfast, we explore “God’s Own Country”, the commercial hub of Kerala with a tour of Cochin visiting the Dutch Palace, the Jewish Synagogue, the St. Francis Church and the Chinese fishing nets. The afternoon we board a housboat for a cruise through the paddy fields, narrow canals and channels of Kerala. Explore Backwaters and meals on the Houseboat. Overnight on House Boat. (B, L, D)

Day 6: Alleppey - Kumarakom (35Km/ 1Hr)

Breakfast at the Hotel. After breakfast, disembark at Alleppey and proceed for Kumarakom, the ideal place to experience the fascinating lifestyles of the area. The accommodation consists of individual cottages built in the traditional Kerala style, relying heavily on wood. Some cottages have private pools. Check In at the Resort. (B, D)

Day 7: Kumarakom / Cochin Airport (80Km/ 2Hrs)

After breakfast, check out from hotel and transfer towards the Cochin Airport for connecting flight to your home. Your wonderful vacation comes to an end! Proceed to Cochin International Airport to connect to your onward flight, with wonderful memories to be cherished for a lifetime. (B)
