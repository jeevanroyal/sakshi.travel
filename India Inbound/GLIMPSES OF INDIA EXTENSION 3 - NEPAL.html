Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi - Kathmandu

Breakfast at the Hotel. After Breakfast proceed to the Airport and depart to Kathmandu. Arrive Kathmandu. Welcome to your ‘Nepal’ tour! Arrive into Kathmandu International Airport. Post customs and immigration, you will be met just outside the arrival hall and transferred to your hotel. The rest of the day is at leisure on your own. Overnight stay at the hotel. (D)

Day 2: In Kathmandu - Full Day City tour of Kathmandu

Today, enjoy a city tour of Kathmandu, visiting temples of Pashupatinath, Boudhanath, Swayambhunath, the temple of the living Goddess and stroll through the old Durbar Square. The rest of the evening is free to enjoy exploring the markets, pubs and casinos on your own. Overnight stay at the hotel. (B, D)

Day 3: On to Pokhara (200 kms / approx. 6 hour drive)

Today you will proceed on a picturesque drive to the enchanting city of Pokhara. On arrival, check into your hotel. The rest of the evening is free to enjoy the serenity and magnificence of the Himalayas or enjoy a leisurely walk around the serene Phewa Lake. Overnight stay at the hotel. (B, D)

Day 4: In Pokhara | Half Day City tour of Pokhrara

Today after Breakfast enjoy a Half Day visit to Bindabasini Temple --The white pagoda-styled building of the Hindu Bindabasini Temple quietly sits at some 3000 feet above sea level along the renowned Annapurna and Machhpuchhare mountain ranges. It is located near the busy Old Bazaar of Pokhara, a center of commerce in the Himalayas and the Gupteshwar Cave where the Shiva Lingam is preserved and finally visit the Devi Falls. Afternoon return to Hotel. Rest of the day free for leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel. (B, D)

Day 5: On to Kathmandu (200 kms / approx. 6 hour drive)

Today drive back to Kathmandu. On arrival, check into the hotel. Rest of the evening is at leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel. (B, D)

Day 6: Kathmandu – Delhi

Breakfast at the Hotel. After Breakfast Proceed to the Airport for your flight to Delhi. Arrive Delhi. Return to the Hotel for an overnight stay. (B, D)

Day 7: Depart Delhi

Breakfast at the Hotel. After Breakfast check out from the hotel.Your wonderful vacation comes to an end! Proceed to Delhi International Airport to connect to your onward flight, with wonderful memories to be cherished for a lifetime. (B)
